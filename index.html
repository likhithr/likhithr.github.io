About Me

My name is Likhith Doddi and I am currently a freshman at the State University of New York at Buffalo (UB). My major will be computer science with a minor in business management. I chose that particular combination for a reason. I have always had a keen interest in computers ever since my family got its first PC back in 2006. Since then I have developed my knowledge regarding how systems work, especially in regards to software. As much as I think that the field of computer science and software are perfect, there seems to be one slight downside. What I mean is that it is sometimes difficult to progress within a software company to play a more managerial role. Don’t get me wrong, programing is the core of any software company but the management side plays a great role on how that information is used. This is why I hope to start off as a programmer and work my way up the business chain and maybe even become a CEO of a company one day. Yeah, I might be reaching there.

Besides my future goals, one important thing about me is my passion for photography. I have experience taking pictures in DSLR’s, point and shoot cameras and even film cameras sometimes. However lately, I have been using my iPhone to capture some of my best shots yet. Check out my collection in the My Gallery section.
